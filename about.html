Pietro Groff,
photographer raised in Italy and fled to Berlin to study photography at the “Neue Schule fur Fotografie - Berlin”. Now living in between Berlin and Milan, shooting global campaigns through the unique aesthetics that only the mix of these two cultures can give. The underground-ish, rebel and punk-ish Berlin vibe meets the elegance and the class typical of Milan. His photography is rooted in coloured, dreamy and moody stories, where his cinematic aesthetic gives them unique atmospheres. Human emotions and fluid storytellings are what characterise more his personality and workflow. In love with small antiques, mostly shoot art and fashion photography.
